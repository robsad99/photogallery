Softball Fall League Game

Briana Harbison is lined up in left field for Randolph College in the 5th inning. They trail the home Hokies 5-0 in this exhibition game.

Beamer Way Flowers

These pink, beautiful flowers light up road across Beamer Way as you head towards Lane Stadium. They fall in front of the Lane Stadium sign that reads "Worsham Field."

Lane Stadium Sign

This is the classic sign made out of patented Hokie Stone. Visitors know that when they read this sign, they will soon enter the terror dome, with no hope of escaping with a victory.

Frank Beamer Statue

You can not think of Hokie football without newly inducted Hall of Fame coach Frank Beamer. This statue was unveiled before kickoff vs the Notre Dame Fighting Irish. Hokie fans, friends, and families swarmed to get a picture with the new addition.

Halloween Delight

Spooky times arrive in Blacksburg and Kroger is in the holiday spirit with their collection of pristine pumpkins outside. They have become a staple for the community this time of year, getting kids excited for Fall.

Sunrise

Hiking is one of the favorite pasttimes for Hokie students. There is nothing like the view looking off a mountain as the sun begins to shine it's glowing essence.

Pivotal Pylons

Straight across from Torgenson Bridge lies the core values etched on what is now one of the most known places on campus. They read brotherhood, honor, leadership, sacrifice, service, loyalty, duty, and UT Prosim. Each student at Virginia Tech is expected to embody these values.

Football Helmet

The Beamer-Lawson practice facility always draws interest from all football fans alike. This helmet is on a wall inside the facility and is shown during a tour of the newly named building.

Duck Pond Sunset

Not much elicits more romance for the Hokie faithful than the Duck Pond. Legend says if your first date is there, the couple will get married, and this sunset and flowers sure help that plan go into motion.

Burruss Flowers

More pretty flowers are prevalent across the campus of Virginia Tech. These can be found outside of Burruss Hall, where most students await the arrival of their chosen bus.

Hokie Slushie

Football games early in the year, while fun, can also be very hot. Most VT fans turn their attentions to the maroon and orange slushies sold at concessions to cool them off while they cheer on their team to victory.

Horticulture Koi

The Hahn Horticulture Garden may not be popular with everyone, but the Koi fish sure do enjoy it. There are many small ponds scattered throughout that inhabit the Japeanese species and provide it comfort.
